About YFM

Your Farm Market started by Colleen McKay is a growing success story. Left to right, Alex Brown, Jennifer Arnott, and owner Colleen McKay. (TARA BOWIE / SENTINEL-REVIEW / QMI AGENCY)

Your Farm Market (YFM) brings local produce to Woodstock. Owner Colleen McKay knows the names of the farmers, the state of the produce and how the season is going. She is committed to finding the freshest fruit and vegetables to bring to you. But that's not all!

In addition to fresh food, YFM also offers baked goods from Anna Mae's bakery and restaurant in Millbank. When you pick up your order, consider visiting the Feel Good Food Truck which serves lunches and dinners.

YFM is open yearly from June 1st to October 31st. Hours are 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, seven days a week.
